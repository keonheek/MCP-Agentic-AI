English: Korean D2C ops integrator that wires Channel Talk + Cafe24 + Naver SmartStore + Kakao 알림톡 + Make.com on n8n self-host, with PIPA-ready setup, on a ₩200K-₩500K/월 retainer.

Position = integrator, not platform. SaaS vendors structurally cannot occupy this (lock-in incentive prevents cross-vendor deep integration).

## 8 mistakes to avoid

- Race-to-bottom 진입가 ₩20-100K (Boost, AutoHunting, 딸깍코딩): margin = 0
- Horizontal "any automation" SKU (바티AI, 일잘러심대리, 이지워크): commodity, cannot win SEO
- Pivoting to consulting / courses (퀀텀점프클럽, 지피터스, visionpulse): implementation margin disappears
- 100% hidden pricing (AI BRIDGE): SMB never contacts
- Single-tool lock-in (Make only) (리부티너): exposed when n8n migration needed
- Naver Blog-only, no own domain (visionpulse): algorithm risk
- Building own LLM (Saltlux, Upstage territory): capital game, boutiques die
- Big4 imitation (anonymous team, RFP-only) (NobleProg Korea): kills SMB trust
