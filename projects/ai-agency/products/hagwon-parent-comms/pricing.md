## Agency 4-tier ladder (authoritative)

| Tier | Setup | Monthly | Scope | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| DEMO | ₩500K | none | 1 scenario, 5-day delivery | Lead generator only |
| STARTER | ₩2M | ₩200K | 3-5 scenarios, Make to n8n migration, 14-day delivery | Real revenue tier |

| D2C STACK + PIPA Tier P | ₩8M | ₩1M | Above + DB isolation + access control + audit | Large chains only |

DO NOT lead with ₩300K + ₩100K/월 in cold DM. That is the product-specific wedge for existing STARTER clients.

## Retainer math for sales conversations

"₩100K/월 retainer = 원장님 2시간 인건비 절약 = 월 1회 학부모 이탈 방지시 손익분기."

매월 학생 1명 이탈 방지 (평균 ₩30K-50K/월 수강료) = retainer 원금 회수.

## Discount rules

- First 3 학원 clients: ₩50K setup discount (position as "학원 얼리어답터 특가")
- Bundle with hagwon-tuition-billing: ₩100K setup discount on combined
- Annual prepay: 2 months free (10 months price for 12 months service)

## Never quote below

- ₩100K/월 recurring (below this, support load exceeds margin)
- ₩300K setup (below this, onboarding effort is not covered)
