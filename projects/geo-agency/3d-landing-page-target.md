# 3D Landing Page — Target Selection

_Research date: 2026-03-25_

## Role of the 3D Landing Page (Updated 2026-03-25)

The 3D landing page is **portfolio + lead magnet**, not a product to sell. Lifestyle SMEs (nail salons, cafes) are NOT paying GEO clients — their customers use Naver Map, not ChatGPT. GEO optimization has no direct ROI for them.

**Use the 3D page for:**
1. Instagram portfolio posts — visual proof of capability
2. Cold outreach hook to get responses ("I built this for free")
3. Door-opener before pitching the actual GEO audit to Tier 1 clients

**Do not:** pitch GEO retainers to nail salons or cafes as primary clients.

---

## Recommended Niche for 3D Demos: 동네 네일샵 / 소규모 카페

**Why (as demo targets, not paying clients):**
- Visual business = dramatic before/after contrast
- Owner-operated — responds on Instagram DMs
- Relies on Naver Blog only — 3D demo is a clear upgrade visually

**Second choice:** 한복 대여, 공방 (craft studio)

## How to Find the Target (10 minutes on Instagram)

**Search these hashtags on Instagram:**

| Hashtag | What you'll find |
|---------|-----------------|
| `#동네네일` | Neighborhood nail shops, local feel |
| `#홍대네일` `#신촌네일` `#건대네일` | Area-specific — lower competition than Gangnam |
| `#자영업네일` | Self-employed nail artists — owner-operated |
| `#네일아트일상` | DIY-adjacent, often small shops |
| `#네일디자인공유` | Small shops sharing work |

**Filter to:** 500–5,000 followers (not a chain). Check the bio link — if there's no link, or it points to a Naver Blog, that's the target.

---

## Evaluation Checklist (30 seconds per account)

- [ ] Follower count: 500–5,000
- [ ] Bio link: missing, or points to a Naver blog / 카카오채널 (NOT a real website)
- [ ] Posts: photos of nail work, not generic stock art (= owner-operated)
- [ ] Location tag: Seoul or nearby — easier to build credibility as local consultant
- [ ] Last post: within 30 days (business still active)

**If 4/5 pass → this is your target.**

---

## What to Build

Once target is identified, proceed to `loop4_3d_landing_page.py`:

1. Screenshot their current Naver blog (the "before")
2. Build a Three.js homepage mockup in ~2 hours (nail portfolio grid, floating thumbnails, soft parallax)
3. Record 30-second screen recording: "before" (Naver blog) → "after" (3D mockup)
4. DM on Instagram: "제 포트폴리오용으로 무료 홈페이지 만들어 드릴 수 있어요. 후기만 부탁드려요 :)"
