# GEO Agency — Korean SME Client Business

_Started: 2026-03-12_
_Status: Research complete. Ready for first free audit._

---

## Concept

A freelance/consulting operation offering **Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)** to Korean SMEs — making their content discoverable and citable by AI systems (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini).

When someone asks an AI "what's the best Korean kimchi brand?" or "top Korean accounting firm in Suwon?" — GEO-optimized content gets cited. This is the AI-era version of SEO.

---

## Market Validation (2026-03-12)

Source: `research/2026-03-11-korean-sme-ai-consulting-market.md`

| Signal | Data |
|--------|------|
| Korean SMEs | 8M+ (99.9% of all enterprises) |
| AI adoption rate | **31%** (well below large enterprises) |
| Want AI consulting | **21.9%** of SMEs = ~1.75M addressable |
| Government AI subsidy | 140B KRW AX Sprint Track (2026) — offsets 30-70% of fees |
| GEO competition | Near zero — no Korean firm has systematized it |
| Seoul vs. regional | Seoul: 40% adoption, outside Seoul: 18% — less competition regionally |

**Key finding:** The gap between "want AI help" and "have access to affordable AI consulting" is large. Big consulting (McKinsey, Deloitte) serves enterprises only. Korean IT giants (Samsung SDS, LG CNS) price for enterprise. Government programs cover planning but not implementation.

**GEO specifically:** No competitor is offering it. First-mover opportunity — but requires market education. SMEs don't know what GEO is; they know they want more customers.

## Service Offering

### Tier 1 — GEO Audit (Entry Point)
- **Price:** 500K–1M KRW
- **Deliverable:** 1-2 page PDF report + 3 specific AI-indexable content changes
- **Proof of value:** Before/after: "when someone asks ChatGPT for your product, here's what they see"
- **Time to complete:** 2-3 hours
- **First one:** Free (for SDC connection) — to produce a case study

### Tier 2 — Monthly GEO Retainer
- **Price:** 1–3M KRW/month
- **Deliverable:** Ongoing content optimization, AI citation monitoring, monthly report
- **Upsell from:** Paid audit clients who see results

### Tier 3 — Agentic Workflow Automation
- **Price:** 5–20M KRW one-time project
- **Deliverable:** Automate 1-2 SME workflows using AI (e.g., email triage, FAQ chatbot, report generation)
- **Target:** Digitally mature SMEs who've done the GEO work and want more
- **Keonhee's moat:** FinAgent + LangGraph + RAG + DART MCP — can build these
