_Created: 2026-03-25 by /execute-next_

## Target Business Type

**Category:** 동네 카페 (neighborhood cafe) or 소규모 베이커리 (small bakery)
**Location:** 서울 — residential neighborhoods (마포구, 성동구, 은평구, 성북구, etc.)
**Instagram followers:** 500–5,000
**Web presence:** Dead link, Modoo site (terminated June 2025), basic Wix, or no website at all

---

## Why This Target

1. **Modoo opportunity** — Naver Modoo was terminated June 26, 2025. Any Korean business with a `*.modoo.at` link on Instagram now has NO website. These are ideal cold outreach targets: they know they need a site, they just haven't moved.
2. **Cafe/bakery visual appeal** — 3D homepage mockups are most dramatic for businesses with beautiful products. Cafe/bakery = strong before/after.
3. **Instagram-first businesses** — Small cafes operate almost entirely on Instagram. They have photos, brand identity, and followers, but no website. A 3D landing page is a clear upgrade.

---

## How to Find a Real Target (5-minute search)

1. Open Instagram, search: `서울 카페` or `마포 카페` or `홍대 카페`
2. Filter to accounts with **500–5,000 followers**
3. Check their bio for a website link
4. Click the link — if it's dead (`modoo.at`) or redirects to a blank Wix page = **perfect target**
5. Screenshot their Instagram and current website (or "no website")
